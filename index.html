About Us

The Best IT Solution With Years of Experience

Company name is Lammbtech (PTY) LTD, a level 1 BBBEE contributor. The company was founded in year 2014 by Mashabane Jackson Legodi who later joint forces with Constance Mamoloko Kganyago, a female non-executive share holder. Lammbtech former registered name is Lammatseleng Business Technology (PTY) LTD and later in 2018 the name changed. The company has two official offices in Polokwane Limpopo and Johannesburg Gauteng and we are ready to grow footprint across all provinces in South Africa and internationally.

Lammbtech is a technology partner of an American based company called Zion tech Group where we provide IT solutions to their high profiled clients based in South Africa, we have done IT support and maintenance to companies such as Nokia, Dimension Data ISP Internet Solutions, Mega Tech, ISS-Shipping, Nielsen Marketing and our partnership is ongoing.
